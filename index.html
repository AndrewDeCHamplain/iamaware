Poor nutrition causes nearly half of the deaths of children under five. Thats around 3.1 million children each year.

Over the past 6 years the number of undernourished people in developped countries increased by 3 million!

In 2010, 48.8 million Americans lived in food insecure households, 32.6 million adults and 16.2 million children.

Globally, there are more than 1 billion overweight adults, at least 300 million of them clinically obese.

About Us

I Am Aware is a group of individuals who are dedicated to informing others on the prevalence of
world hunger and malnutrition by bringing the facts close to home. We encourage others to be aware
and contribute to the efforts existing locally and globally.
